# Explore the tutorial

Inside `nicegeo-walkthrough-tutorial.ncg`:

- Move the cursor through the **tactic proofs** and watch **Proof State**.
- **Hover** global and local names; **F12** on symbols for **go to definition**.

When you are done, you can exit Proof Mode from the Command Palette (**NiceGeo: Exit Proof Mode**) or the status bar.
